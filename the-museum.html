Admission

The entrance is free for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps.

It also has audio guides in all European languages and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

We strive to provide equal access to our venue to all types of audiences. If you have any suggestions please contact us here

Food & drinks

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more. Our menu is suitable for all types of diets.

Get in touch with our friendly staff if you have any allergies.

shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
